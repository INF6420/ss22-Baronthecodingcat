Anderson

A place holder for a brief family history. The Anderson traveled from near Frederickstad, Norway to Minnesota shortly after the birth of their twins, Andrew and John M. Anderson. Tracing the twins makes it easier to pick up the trail of the family than otherwise might have been the case with such a common name.
